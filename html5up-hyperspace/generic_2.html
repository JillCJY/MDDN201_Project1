What is big data?

Big data is a field that can analyze the huge amount of complex data, which traditional data processing method cannot deal with. There are three key concepts of big data, which are volume, variety, and velocity. For turning into messy information into readable and use for data, it requires the operator can capturing data, data storage, data analysis, search, sharing, transfer, visualization, querying, updating, information privacy and data source. The most common usage of big data is to predict user behavior by collecting and analyzing their data.

.

What can big data do?

Stream Media

One of the big suppliers of big data is stream media platform. The user should notice that when they sign up for the website or download the application of that, it would offer them the Terms of Service agreements, which will allow the site to store, collect and share the service data and use them in a suitable way. In most cases, the users are willing them to do that. Therefore, the operators of the website can statistic data of the input though the site search engine to predict users preferences and promote appropriate ads base on that.

Store

Have you noticed that when we are shopping at a chain store, the consultant of that store will inquire you to join the membership free with a card and encourage you to use it every time you shop there? The use of the loyalty card is for not only rewarding repeat customers and incentivizes more shopping, but also for tracing and storing users’ purchase data, which allow them to build detailed customer profile. So that they can use it to modified their product types and even can sell those data to the other companies, for example advertisers.

Education

Modern education system cannot be separated with online platform. Almost every education institution has their own website for both propagation and teaching. Students can use the resources from the website to help them with studying. For example, school can use the Turnitin to evaluate students’ essays and researches that the website can compare the student’s article with other thousands of articles, which has been published online to see student’s academic credibility. In the other word, to check is the student copied the content from others without any references.
