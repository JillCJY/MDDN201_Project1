Introduction

As the improvement of network coverage, the amount of information, which enable be exchanged through telecommunication network has dramatically increased from 281 petabytes in 1986 to 667 Exabyte annually in 2014, which can create huge amount of data with enormous value.

As people pay more and more attention to big data, the big data technologies has been applied in every corner in our life. Therefore, for following the trend of times, everyone should have a basic understanding about the operation principles of big data. To those people who have find the potential values of big data and want to use it to build a business, it is a good chance to learn more about big data, which can help them to broaden the horizon and be familiar with the common business models of big data.

.

Target Audience

The target audience for this project is to all the people in the society. Especially to those people who has zero base of big data, also the group of people who want to use big data to create either business or academic value. This project is going to introduce the basic operating principles of the big data, which including the method that companies collect data from us and turn those messy data into the information with commercial both academic value. For engaging the audiences and make them easier to understand it, this project will introduce the big data form three aspects, which are stream media, the chain stores and education.
